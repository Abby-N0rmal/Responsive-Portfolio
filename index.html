About Me

Bob Ross Lipsum... If you didn't have baby clouds, you wouldn't have big clouds. We'll have a super time. And I know you're saying, 'Oh Bob, you've done it this time.' And you may be right. A happy cloud.

Think about a cloud. Just float around and be there. We don't have to be concerned about it. We just have to let it fall where it will. A tree needs to be your friend if you're going to paint him. Use absolutely no pressure. Just like an angel's wing. In your world you have total and absolute power.
